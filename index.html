REITs: The Future of Real Assets Investing

Overview

If you wish to learn all about REITs, specifically in the context of India, you have come to the right place. Books, Blogs, Videos and Training. Its all here. We connect the dots.

REITs and InvITs are acronyms for “Real Estate Investment Trusts” and “Infrastructure Investment Trusts” respectively. They enable you to invest in commercial real estate or infrastructure assets for as little as under INR 500! You can buy and sell REITs just like normal equity stocks through your trading account. However the returns, taxation, margins etc. are different for REITs and InvITs vis-a-vis normal equity stocks and some amount of learning can prove immensely useful.
