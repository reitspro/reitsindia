About Gaurav Jain

Gaurav is a trainer, practitioner of real estate finance and an author of two books on his subject. His book “REITs and InVits: Whats new in the Indian stock markets” is a Primer of the Unique Financial Financial Instruments for Realty Assets His ealier book titled Real Estate Investment & Financial Analysis or REIFA is an awarded book and a bestseller in its category in India. It was meant for readers interested in a perspective on financing and related analysis of real estate investments.

Gaurav's training expertize lies in the subject of real estate with a focus on investment and financial aspects for realty assets. He has conducted training sessions for participants of courses in various private and government institutions such as the NSE Academy (a subsidiary of National Stock Exchange), HUDCO-HSMI, IFAN, AAFM etc. Having spent time in both finance sector as well as real estate industry (a short period in investment banking followed by nearly two decades in real estate industry) he has a unique background to enable him to analyze the subject of this book and do justice to the same. A scholar throughout his academic years, Gaurav has completed his schooling from Delhi Public School, R.K. Puram and holds an honors degree in commerce from Hans Raj College, Delhi University. In line with his passion of linking finance to real estate industry, his post-graduate qualifications include both finance and real estate - he is a Chartered Financial Analyst, (CFA, ICFAI) as well as an Urban Planner (PGDUPDL).

About Elesh Kariya

Elesh comes with a decade of versatile professional experience across investment banking and corporate finance advising companies in real estate, retail and consumer sectors. He has advised on fund raise done by leading real estate developers and consumer brands in the capital markets including IPOs of all REITs and Public InvITs in India. Elesh is currently leading the strategy function at Homexchange Ltd and has been part of Axis Capital, Motilal Oswal, Housing.com and Samsung India. He has earned Masters in Finance from Narsee Monjee Mumbai and Bachelors in Computer Science from NIT Bhopal.
